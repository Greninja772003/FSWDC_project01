My Top 5 All-Time Hindi Movie Suggestion

  1. Phir Hera Pheri

    This movie is full of joy and comedy

  2. Drishyam

    This movie is a thrilling drama about a man who goes to great lengths to protect his family

  3. Agneepath

    This movie is about a young boy who seeks revenge on the mobster who murdered his father

  4. Gangs of Wasseypur

    This movie is about rise of the Khan crime family in the coal mining town of Wasseypur

  5. Jolly LLB-2

    This movie is about a clumsy lawyer who takes on a critical court case
